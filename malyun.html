Welcome to Cat Cuddles, where you can find your nearest cat cafe, and cat adoption center!

Our Mission:

Cat Cuddles was started by Malyun Abdullahi who is a cat aficionado. She loves going to cat cafes and believes cats (and other animals) who are kept in possibly bad living conditions should be taken care of. She also believes cats with injuries really deserve a home. Our mission is to help others find the cats of their dreams with a cat adoption center and cat cafe locator to find the nearest place to adopt your cat. Animal cruelty is so prevalant in the world, adopt neglected cats now!
